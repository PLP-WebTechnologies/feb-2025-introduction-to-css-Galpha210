About Kevin De Bruyne

Kevin De Bruyne is a Belgian professional footballer who plays as a midfielder for Premier League club Manchester City and the Belgium national team.

Born on June 28, 1991, in Drongen, Belgium, De Bruyne began his career at Genk before moving to Chelsea in 2012. After a loan spell at Werder Bremen, he joined VfL Wolfsburg in 2014, where he won the Bundesliga Player of the Year award.

He is a Manchester City midfielder who has had a very successful career at the club. He joined the club from Chelsea Football Club where he was considered as a flop.

Kevin De Bruyne is known for his exceptional passing, vision, and playmaking abilities. He has been a key player for Manchester City since joining the club in 2015, helping them win multiple Premier League titles and domestic cups.

De Bruyne has also been a standout performer in the UEFA Champions League, contributing significantly to Manchester City's success in the tournament. His ability to create goal-scoring opportunities and his leadership on the pitch have made him one of the most respected midfielders in world football.

A versatile and highly talented player in the media, he plays mainly as an attacking or a central midfielder but can also operate in several other positions and has been used as a winger or second striker. He has even been deployed in a deeper creative midfield position and on occasion, a box-to-box role or as a false 9. He is often described by pundits as one of the best and most complete modern day advanced playmakers due to his technique, skill, athleticism, work-rate off the ball, vision, crossing accuracy, wide range of passing, and powerful long-range shooting ability with either foot.
